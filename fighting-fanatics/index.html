Player HP: 150
Turn: 1


How to Play


When there is an enemy on screen, press fight to hit that enemy. If there are multiple, then it will hit in reading order (top left to bottom right).
If you are low on HP or have no enemies, heal to recover HP. There's no limit!
Running will lose all progress and you will start back on turn 1.
When you defeat an enemy, you will gain stats!
